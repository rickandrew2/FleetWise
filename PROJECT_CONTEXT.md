# FleetWise — Project Context

> **In one sentence:** FleetWise helps Philippine logistics companies stop guessing about their fuel costs and start making informed decisions — by tracking every fill-up, scoring every driver, alerting on problems automatically, and delivering clean reports without anyone having to manually pull the numbers together.

---

## What is FleetWise?

FleetWise is a **web-based management system** for companies that own and operate a fleet of vehicles — think delivery companies, logistics businesses, trucking operations, or any organization that runs multiple vehicles day-to-day.

---

## What Problem Does It Solve?

Imagine you run a logistics company in the Philippines with 20 trucks. Every day those trucks go out on deliveries. Every few days they stop to refuel. At the end of the month you look at your fuel expenses and think — **"why did we spend this much?"**

You have no idea:
- Which truck is guzzling the most fuel
- Which driver is being careless or inefficient
- Which routes are costing you more than they should
- Whether a truck is due for maintenance and running poorly because of it

Most small and medium logistics companies in the Philippines track all of this in **Excel spreadsheets or paper logbooks** — or not at all. FleetWise replaces that mess with a proper system.

---

## What Does It Actually Do?

### 💰 It tracks every peso spent on fuel
Every time a truck fills up at a gas station, that fill-up gets recorded — how many liters, how much it cost, which station, which driver, which vehicle. Over time you can see exactly where your fuel budget is going.

### 📊 It tells you if a driver is wasting fuel
Not all fuel spending is equal. A driver who takes a longer route, drives aggressively, or idles too long will burn more fuel than expected. FleetWise compares how much fuel a trip *should* have used versus how much it *actually* used — and gives each driver an efficiency score. The higher the score, the more fuel they're wasting.

### 🚨 It automatically warns you when something is wrong
You don't have to check every log manually. FleetWise watches the data for you and fires an alert when:
- A driver burns significantly more fuel than expected on a trip
- A single fill-up costs unusually high
- Someone fills more liters than the tank can even hold (possible fraud or data error)
- A vehicle hasn't been serviced and is overdue for maintenance

### 📄 It generates reports automatically
Every week and every month, FleetWise automatically generates a report — a proper PDF or Excel file — that summarizes your fleet's total fuel cost, which vehicles cost the most, which drivers are most and least efficient, and what alerts were triggered. You can hand this directly to a company owner or operations manager.

### 🖥️ It gives you a live dashboard
The moment you open FleetWise you see the big picture — total fuel cost this month, your fleet's overall efficiency, how many active alerts need attention, and a chart showing whether your costs are going up or down over the past 6 months.

## Who Is It For?

| Person | What they get from FleetWise |
|---|---|
| **Company Owner** | Monthly cost reports, knows exactly what fleet operations cost |
| **Fleet Manager** | Daily visibility — which vehicles and drivers need attention |
| **Driver** | Logs their own fill-ups and trips, sees their own efficiency score |

---

## Why Does This Matter Right Now?

Fuel prices in the Philippines have been rising significantly due to global tensions. For a logistics company running 20+ vehicles, even a 10% reduction in fuel waste can mean hundreds of thousands of pesos saved per year. FleetWise gives fleet managers the visibility they need to actually achieve that.

---

## The Five Core Features

| Feature | What it does in plain terms |
|---|---|
| **Vehicle Registry** | Keep a record of every vehicle in the fleet with its fuel efficiency rating |
| **Fuel Log Tracking** | Record every fill-up — liters, cost, station, driver, date |
| **Route & Efficiency Scoring** | Score every trip — did the driver use more or less fuel than expected? |
| **Automatic Alerts** | Get notified automatically when something looks wrong |
| **Automated Reports** | Weekly and monthly PDF/Excel reports generated without lifting a finger |

---

## What FleetWise Is NOT

- It is **not a GPS tracker** — it does not track vehicles in real time
- It is **not a payroll system** — it scores efficiency but does not compute driver pay
- It is **not a maintenance booking system** — it alerts when maintenance is due but does not schedule it
- It is **not a consumer app** — it is built for business operations, not individual drivers finding cheap gas

---

## The Story Behind It

The inspiration came from a real observation — fuel prices in the Philippines have been climbing steadily, putting pressure on logistics and delivery businesses that run on tight margins. A fleet of 20 vehicles spending even ₱500 extra per fill-up per week adds up to over ₱500,000 in unnecessary costs per year. Most of these businesses have no system to catch this waste early. FleetWise was built to change that.
